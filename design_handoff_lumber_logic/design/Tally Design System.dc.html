04 — Responsive

Phone patterns

Three tiers: phone ≤760px (designed down to 375), tablet 761–1099px, desktop above. On phones the top nav becomes a bottom tab bar, grand totals pin above it as a Walnut strip, and the tally worksheet restacks to one row per length with −/+ steppers. On tablets the worksheet keeps its horizontal grid but folds the side panels into a header row and a Walnut summary strip. Landscape phones (height ≤480px) unstick the header and drop tab labels to icons so the worksheet keeps the screen.

Bottom tab bar

Fixed, three tabs, Paper surface with a hairline top border. Active tab in Timber 600; 44px+ targets; safe-area padding below.
